Zoom School

An overwheling an familiar view of the internet. This is my take on the new norm and what is happening in the classroom of today. I use archival gifs, bright colors, and cocophonous surplus to evoke a feeling of early internet mania.

PVD Tunnel

A collaborative project investigating the physical site of Providenc Rhode Islands single abandoned train tunnel. I and my colleagues explored, investigated, and mapped the space ultimately resulting in a large poster like map detailing not only the history, and physical attributes, but the human experiences of the past present and future.

Quarentine Colaborative

A collaborative publication reflecting on a semester of design work within a quarentined life. We showcased our recent projects using the vehicle of our desktop, having become our new workshop, and gallery in the age of COVID 19. Each page of this digital publication features a chatbox of our feedback, mimicking the would be gallery space dialogues.
